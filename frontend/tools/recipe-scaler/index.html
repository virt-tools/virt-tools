Recipe Scaler

Resize a recipe for a different number of servings. Paste your ingredient list, set the original and desired servings, and each leading quantity is scaled by the ratio. Integers, decimals, and fractions (1/2, 1 1/2) all parse; scaled results render as clean fractions where possible, and lines without a number pass through unchanged. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Only the leading number on each line is scaled; the rest of the line is kept verbatim, so 2 cups flour at 2× becomes 4 cups flour. Lines with no leading number (e.g. salt to taste) pass through untouched. Fractions simplify to the smallest denominator up to 8 (0.5→1/2, 0.75→3/4); mixed numbers like 1 1/2 are recognized on input and produced on output. Pluralization isn't changed. Runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
