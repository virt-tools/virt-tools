Favicon Generator

Turn one image into a complete favicon set — a multi-size .ico, Apple touch icon, Android/PWA icons, and a web app manifest — bundled as a single ZIP ready to drop into your site root. Everything is generated locally on a canvas; nothing is uploaded.

Click to choose an image
or drop one here, or paste (Ctrl/⌘+V). A square PNG works best (≥ 512×512).

A square source image of at least 512×512 gives the sharpest results across every size. The .ico bundles the 16, 32 and 48px entries (PNG-embedded, supported by all current browsers); the manifest references the 192 and 512px PNGs for Android/PWA installability, and the 180px PNG is the Apple touch icon. The ZIP uses no compression (PNGs are already compressed), so it's tiny and unzips cleanly anywhere. No network requests are made.
