Click Speed Test (CPS)

Measure how many times you can click per second. Pick a test duration, then click the pad as fast as you can until the timer ends — your clicks-per-second average, peak 1-second burst, and a per-second bar chart are reported. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Choose a duration, then click the pad — the first click starts the timer. Keep clicking as fast as you can until it ends. Average CPS = total clicks ÷ duration. Peak burst is the most clicks you landed within any one-second window. The bar chart shows clicks per second. Gaps between clicks give a consistency figure (lower std dev = steadier rhythm). Common benchmarks: ~6 CPS average, ~8–10 CPS peak for fast clickers. Runs locally — no data leaves your browser.
