CSV Statistics & Summary

Get summary statistics for every column of a CSV — count, null count, unique count, min, max, mean, median, std dev, and most frequent value. Auto-detects numeric vs string columns and the delimiter. Pairs with the CSV Viewer, CSV Filter, and Statistics Calculator. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Per-column statistics

Numeric columns get min, max, mean, median, std dev, and quartiles. String columns get count, null count, unique count, most frequent value, and shortest/longest value. Auto-sniffs the delimiter. Pairs with CSV Viewer and Statistics Calculator. Runs locally.
