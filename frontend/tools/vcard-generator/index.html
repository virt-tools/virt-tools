vCard Generator

Build a vCard (electronic business card) from contact fields and download it as a .vcf file or scan an optional QR code to import it on a phone. Produces a vCard 3.0 file (broadly compatible) with CRLF line endings, 75-octet folding, and the required escaping of commas, semicolons, and newlines. Everything runs locally in your browser — your contact details never leave the page.
