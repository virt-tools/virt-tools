Periodic Table

All 118 chemical elements in an interactive table, colour-coded by category. Click an element for its details, or search by name, symbol, or number. Runs locally in your browser.

Click an element to see its details.

Standard periodic table (IUPAC 1–118). Tile colour shows category: alkali metals, alkaline earths, transition metals, post-transition metals, metalloids, nonmetals, halogens, noble gases, lanthanides, and actinides. Atomic mass is the standard atomic weight; for elements with no stable isotope (technetium and everything from polonium onward except a few), the mass number of the longest-lived isotope is shown in brackets. Periods are the seven rows of the main table; the lanthanides (57–71) and actinides (89–103) are pulled out into the two rows below. Click a tile for details, or type to highlight. Runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
