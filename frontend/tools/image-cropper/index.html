Image Cropper

Crop an image by dragging a selection on the canvas — choose an aspect ratio (free, 1:1, 4:3, 16:9, 3:2, 9:16), move or resize it with the corner handles, and download the cropped result as PNG, JPEG or WebP. Everything is processed locally with a canvas; your image never leaves your browser.
