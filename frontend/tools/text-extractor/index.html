Text Pattern Extractor

Paste text and pull out everything that matches a built-in pattern — emails, URLs, IPv4 / IPv6 addresses, phone numbers, hex colors, hashtags, @mentions, dates, times, credit-card-shaped numbers, MAC addresses, and UUIDs — or use your own regular expression. Deduplicate, sort, count, and copy. Runs entirely in your browser.

Built-in patterns are heuristic and may produce false positives or miss unusual formats — verify important matches. Phone numbers use a flexible international pattern; credit-card-shaped numbers are not validated against Luhn. Runs locally.
