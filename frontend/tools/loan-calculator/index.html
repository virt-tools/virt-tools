Loan & Amortization Calculator

Compute the monthly payment on a fixed-rate loan and see the full amortization schedule — how each payment splits between interest and principal, month by month, until the balance hits zero. Optionally add a down payment and an extra monthly principal payment to see how much interest you save. Everything is calculated locally in your browser.

Loan amount = purchase price − down payment. Term may also be entered as months by switching the unit.
