Quadratic Equation Solver

Solve a quadratic equation of the form ax² + bx + c = 0. Enter the three coefficients and the solver computes the discriminant, the roots (two distinct real, one repeated real, or a complex-conjugate pair), the vertex and axis of symmetry of the parabola, and the factored form when the roots are real. A built-in check evaluates the polynomial at each root so you can confirm the result is correct. Everything runs locally in your browser.
