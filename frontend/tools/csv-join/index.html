CSV Join & Merge

Join two CSV tables on a common key column — inner, left, right, or full outer join — like SQL JOIN for CSV files. Pick the key column from each side, choose the join type, and copy or download the merged result. Right-side column names are suffixed to avoid collisions; rows that don't match are handled per the join type. Handles quoted fields, embedded commas, and newlines. Everything runs locally in your browser.
