Pixel Art Editor

Draw pixel art in your browser on an adjustable grid. Pick a colour, paint cells by clicking or dragging, erase, or flood-fill a region. Export the finished art as a PNG or a scalable SVG. Everything stays local — your canvas never leaves your browser.

Paint with click or drag; the Fill tool flood-fills the connected region of the same colour under the cursor. Erase clears a cell. The grid is square (8–32 cells per side); PNG exports at the displayed resolution, SVG is scalable and includes only the filled cells over a dark background. Runs locally.
