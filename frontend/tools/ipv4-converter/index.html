IPv4 Address Converter

Convert an IPv4 address between dotted-decimal, integer (decimal), hexadecimal, binary, octal, and the in-addr.arpa reverse-DNS name. Enter an address in any of these formats and get every representation at once. Pure 32-bit integer math, in your browser — nothing sent over the network.

Dotted-decimal, plain integer, 0x hex, 0b binary, or 0o octal are all auto-detected.
