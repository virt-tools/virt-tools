BMI Calculator

Compute Body Mass Index from your weight and height, in either metric (kilograms and centimetres) or imperial (pounds and feet/inches) units. The result is shown with its WHO adult category (underweight through obesity classes I–III) and a healthy-weight range for your height (BMI 18.5–24.9). BMI is a screening measure for adults and isn't a body-fat or health diagnosis — athletes, the very muscular, children, and pregnant people should use other measures. Everything runs locally in your browser.
