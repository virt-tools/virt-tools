WiFi QR Code Generator

Generate a QR code that connects people to your WiFi network — just point a phone camera at it. Enter your network name (SSID), encryption type, and password, and the QR encodes the standard WIFI: URI that iOS Camera, Android Camera, and most QR readers recognize. Optionally hide the password and mark the network as hidden. Pairs with the QR Code Generator. Everything runs locally in your browser; no network details leave your device.

The QR encodes WIFI:T:WPA;S:mynetwork;P:mypass;H:false;; — the standard format recognized by iOS and Android camera apps. Escape characters (backslash, semicolon, comma, colon) in the SSID and password are escaped with a backslash. Pairs with the QR Code Generator. Runs locally.
