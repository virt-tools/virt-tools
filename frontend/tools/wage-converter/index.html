Wage & Salary Converter

Convert a pay rate between hourly, annual, monthly, weekly, biweekly, semimonthly, and daily amounts, or estimate overtime pay. Enter a rate in any pay period and see the equivalent in every other period, given your hours per week and weeks per year. The overtime calculator works out regular and overtime pay, the weekly and annualized gross, and the effective hourly rate. All figures are pre-tax gross. Pairs with the Compound Interest and Income/Effective Rate tools. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Enter a pay rate to convert.

Annual = hourly × hours/week × weeks/year; monthly = annual / 12; weekly = annual / weeks/year; biweekly = 2 × weekly; semimonthly = annual / 24; daily = annual / (weeks/year × 5) assuming a five-day week. Overtime weekly gross = rate × regular hours + rate × multiplier × overtime hours; annualized = weekly × weeks/year. All amounts are pre-tax gross — taxes and deductions vary by jurisdiction. Everything runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
