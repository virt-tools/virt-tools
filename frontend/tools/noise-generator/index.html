Noise Generator

Play continuous white, pink, or brown noise in your browser — useful for sleep, focus, and masking background sound. White noise is flat across frequencies; pink noise falls at 3 dB per octave (balanced, often preferred for sleep); brown noise falls at 6 dB per octave (deeper, rumbling). Built on Web Audio, runs locally with no network.

Stopped. Start at a low volume to protect your hearing.

White noise: each sample is random and independent — equal energy at every frequency. Pink noise uses a Paul Kellet filter so energy drops 3 dB per octave, sounding balanced. Brown noise integrates white noise for a 6 dB-per-octave drop, a deep rumble. The waveform preview shows a short slice of the generated buffer. Generated locally with Web Audio; nothing is uploaded.
