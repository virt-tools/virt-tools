Pregnancy Due Date Calculator

Estimate a pregnancy due date from the first day of the last menstrual period (LMP) using Naegele's rule (LMP + 280 days / 40 weeks). See the current gestational age, trimester, and a milestone timeline (end of first trimester, anatomy-scan window, viability, full term, and the due date). Runs entirely in your browser — enter only a date, nothing is uploaded.

Enter the first day of your last menstrual period.

Naegele's rule estimates the due date as LMP + 280 days, assuming a 28-day cycle with ovulation around day 14. Gestational age is counted from the LMP, not from conception (typically ~2 weeks later). Trimesters: first 0–12w, second 13–27w, third 28w–birth. Term milestones: full term 37w, due 40w, post-term 42w. This is an estimate for general information only — cycles vary, and only a healthcare provider can date a pregnancy accurately (often via early ultrasound). Runs locally.
