Enter dimensions to see the tile count.

Tiles per axis are counted as ceil(span / (tile + gap)), so every partial tile at an edge rounds up to a whole one. The base count is columns × rows with no spare; to buy adds your waste percentage (rounded up to a whole tile) for cuts and breakage. A 10% extra is typical for straight-laid layouts; diagonal or patterned layouts and natural-stone tiles usually want 15% or more. Coverage area is reported in square feet and square metres.
