Pace Calculator

Convert between pace, time, and distance for running and cycling. Enter any two of distance, pace, and total time, choose the one to solve for, and the third is computed — along with speed in km/h and mph and a cumulative split table. Miles and kilometres convert using the exact definition (1 mile = 1.609344 km), so pace per mile and pace per kilometre stay perfectly consistent. Everything runs locally in your browser.
