Calorie Burn Calculator

Estimate calories burned during physical activity from its MET (metabolic equivalent of task) value, your body weight, and the duration. Choose from a built-in list of common activities (Ainsworth compendium values) or enter a custom MET. The formula is kcal = MET × weight (kg) × time (hours). Estimates are rough — actual burn varies with fitness, intensity, and metabolism. Everything runs locally in your browser.
