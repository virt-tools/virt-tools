Geographic Coordinate Converter

Convert between decimal degrees (e.g. 48.85822) and degrees / minutes / seconds (e.g. 48° 51′ 29.6″ N) for latitude and longitude — with hemisphere handling and range validation. Runs entirely in your browser.

Decimal degrees use the sign for hemisphere (negative = S / W). DMS input accepts °, ', ", spaces or : as separators and an optional trailing N/S/E/W.
