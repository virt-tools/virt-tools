Business Card Designer

Design a printable business card — fill in your details, pick colors and a layout, and export a high-resolution PNG (300 DPI). Everything renders locally in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

—

Renders to an offscreen 300 DPI canvas, then scaled down for the on-screen preview — so what you see is exactly what downloads. Standard bleed/margins aren't applied; leave ~3 mm safe padding around your text when printing. No network requests are made.
