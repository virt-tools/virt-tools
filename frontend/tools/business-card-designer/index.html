Business Card Designer

Design a printable business card — fill in your details, pick colors and a layout, and export a high-resolution PNG (300 DPI). Everything renders locally in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

—

Renders to an offscreen 300 DPI canvas, then scaled down for the on-screen preview — so what you see is exactly what downloads. For print-sheet mode, the cards are centered on a Letter/A4 page at common blank-sheet layouts (Avery 5371/8371/8871, L7413/C32011); registration varies by printer and stock, so test-print one sheet and adjust the gap as needed before a full run. Standard bleed/margins aren't applied; leave ~3 mm safe padding around your text. No network requests are made.
