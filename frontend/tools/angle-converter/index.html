Angle Converter

Convert an angle between degrees, radians, gradians (gons), turns (revolutions), arcminutes, arcseconds, and NATO mils, and to or from degrees-minutes-seconds (DMS). Enter a value in any unit (or DMS) and every other unit is shown. Pairs with the Trigonometry Calculator. Everything runs locally in your browser.

A full circle is 360° = 2π rad = 400 grad = 1 turn = 21,600 arcminutes = 1,296,000 arcseconds = 6,400 NATO mils. DMS uses 60 arcminutes per degree and 60 arcseconds per arcminute. The converter keeps raw values (it does not wrap to a single turn), so 720° is reported as 4π rad / 2 turns. Everything runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
