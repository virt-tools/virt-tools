Slab volume = length × width × thickness (thickness in inches ÷ 12 to feet). Round footings = π × (diameter ÷ 2)² × depth × count. Cubic yards = cubic feet ÷ 27; cubic metres = cubic feet ÷ 35.3147. The waste factor inflates the volume before bag counts are rounded up. Bag yields assume pre-mixed concrete: 40 lb ≈ 0.30 ft³, 60 lb ≈ 0.45 ft³, 80 lb ≈ 0.60 ft³.
