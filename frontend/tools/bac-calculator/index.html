BAC Calculator

Estimate blood alcohol concentration (BAC) from the number of standard drinks, body weight, sex, and time elapsed since you started drinking, using the Widmark formula. Shows BAC in per-mille (‰) and percent (%), an impairment level, common legal driving limits, and an estimate of time until sober. Estimates are approximate — actual BAC varies by metabolism, food, and tolerance. Do not use to decide whether you are safe to drive. Everything runs locally.
