Time Zone Converter

Pick a date, time and source time zone, then see that same instant in cities around the world — with the UTC offset, weekday and day-shift for each. Useful for scheduling calls and meetings across time zones. Conversion uses the browser's built-in Intl support with the standard IANA time-zone database; everything runs locally and no data leaves your device.
