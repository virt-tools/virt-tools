Roof pitch is rise over run, conventionally per 12 inches of horizontal run — so 6/12 rises 6 inches per foot. The angle is atan(rise/run), the percent slope is rise/run × 100, the rafter length per foot of run is sqrt(run² + rise²), and the roof-area multiplier is sec(angle) = sqrt(1 + ratio²) — the factor that turns a flat footprint into actual roof surface (a 6/12 gable needs ~1.118 sq ft of roofing per sq ft of floor). Common pitches: 3/12 low, 4/12–6/12 standard, 8/12–12/12 steep. Runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
