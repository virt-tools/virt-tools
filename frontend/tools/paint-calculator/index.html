Wall area = 2 × (length + width) × height. Door and window counts multiply by their per-opening area (defaults: a 20 ft² door, a 15 ft² window) and you can set a custom opening total for anything else. The ceiling, if included, adds length × width. Paintable area is divided by your paint's coverage (defaults: 350 ft²/gal or 10 m²/L — check your can's label) and multiplied by the number of coats, then rounded up to whole cans of the size you'll buy. This is an estimate; porous walls, strong colour changes, and texture all change how much paint you actually use. Runs locally — nothing leaves your browser.
