Work Hours Calculator

Add up hours worked across clock-in / clock-out entries, with per-entry break deductions and a regular-vs-overtime split. Shifts that cross midnight (an out time earlier than the in time) are handled automatically, breaks can't make a day negative, and you can set the regular-hours threshold per entry (8 hours by default). Totals are shown in hours:minutes and decimal hours. Everything runs locally in your browser.
