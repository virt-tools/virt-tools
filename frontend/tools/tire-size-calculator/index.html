Tire Size & Speedometer Calculator

Decode a tire size like 225/45R17 into its diameter, circumference and revs per mile/km, then compare an old and new tire size to see the speedometer and odometer error and the ride-height change from the swap. Useful before changing wheel or tire sizes so you know how far off your speedo will read and whether the new rubber will clear. Assumes the same load/inflation; real rolling radius is slightly smaller than the geometric radius. Pure arithmetic, in your browser.
